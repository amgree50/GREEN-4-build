About

Sometimes something sweet is all you need to satisfy your cravings, but you don't want to spend hours in the kitchen. This three ingredient peanut butter cookie recipe is just the treat you need. I recommend you put on some tunes and enjoy this simple and delicious treat!

Some tunes to bake to.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup peanut butter
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1 large egg

Recipe

  1. Mix the peanut butter, sugar, and egg together.
  2. Form the dough into balls and flatten them with a fork.
  3. Bake at 350°F for 10 minutes.
  4. ENJOY!
